How to use

  • Drag / one fingerRotate (horizontal) & tilt (vertical)
  • Two-finger scrollPan (up/down, left/right)
  • Mouse wheel / pinchZoom
  • Click a nodeSelect & trace lineage (parents = amber, children = cyan)
  • Search (top right)Jump to a language
  • Legend chips (bottom)Filter by paradigm (⌘/Shift to toggle individually)
  • Controls (above legend)Flip time axis · auto-rotate · time drift · center-on-click
  • EscClose selection & restore the previous view

Height = year · color = paradigm · lines = influence. When left idle, the view slowly drifts down the timeline and loops back to the start.
